Introducing Nagi

A simpler, better and more elegant way of making subtitles

The community deserves a better tool

There are millions people around the world making subtitles everyday for free.

Yet the only tools available for them are limited and outdated.

Until they meet Nagi

Wider and Better Accessibility

Nagi supports both offline and online videos.
In addition, it reads and outputs a bunch of different subtitle flavours.
That includes the lastest html5 webVTT standard.

vtt

srt

ssa

mp4

webm

ogv
